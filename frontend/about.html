About Mars Animal Shelter

Our Mission

Founded in 2138, Mars Animal Shelter is the first interplanetary rescue organization dedicated to the care and protection of Earth species adapting to the Martian environment.

We believe every creature deserves a loving home, even 140 million miles from Earth.

Our Facilities

Located in the climate-controlled Valles Marineris Colony, our state-of-the-art habitat domes provide Earth-like conditions for our animal residents.

Our specialized veterinary team is trained in low-gravity animal care and Martian adaptation protocols.

Our History

When the first Mars colonists discovered the challenges of raising Earth animals in Martian conditions, our founder Dr. Elena Vasquez established the shelter to research and develop solutions.

Today, we care for over 200 animals, from specially-bred Mars dogs to gravity-adapted cats and birds that help bring a touch of Earth to our red planet home.
